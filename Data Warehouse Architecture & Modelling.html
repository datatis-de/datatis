Our Methodology

Requirement Analysis

We begin by thoroughly understanding your business objectives, data sources, and analytical needs to create a solution that delivers real value.

Conceptual Design

Developing high-level models that map business processes to data structures, establishing the framework for your data warehouse.

Logical Modeling

Creating detailed models using industry-standard approaches (Kimball, Inmon, or Data Vault) tailored to your specific requirements.

Physical Implementation

Translating models into optimized database schemas with appropriate indexing, partitioning, and storage strategies.

Architecture Approaches

We employ the most suitable architecture pattern for your needs:

Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW)

A centralized repository integrating data from across your organization, providing a single source of truth.

Data Mart Approach

Department-specific warehouses that can be integrated into a larger architecture as needed.

Hybrid Solutions

Combining the best aspects of different approaches to meet complex business requirements.
